About Claire. Foodie Fanatic!

Hi there I am Claire Summers, I am a food fanatic, I love great tasting foods. I am what you call a foodie, I travel the world and visit all its majors cities. Looking for the best foods out there, allowing my taste buds to enjoy every sensation. I am a classic chef by profession, this journey started when I realized that are so many foods to experiance out there. With this journey I started reviewing and recommending the best restaurants and food outlets out there. So with this you know where to get the best foods in your cities and other countries.

South Africa

Hey Foodie Fanatix! I went on an adventure to south africa and visted Johannesburg. I took my taste buds out for a treat in the city. We visted a few places Restaurants and food outlets. The expierence was phenomenal and the food was divine. South Africa is a country with many hidden places and special foods. Wish I could go more places to eat, but only so much a girl can eat.

Margherita Pizza It'll blow your mind

We went to a restaurants with a 4.5 Star rating from 304 reviews, by the name of Ristorante-La-Trinita which is an Italian owned, This restaurant has the original Italian look and feel. They serve pizza and some amazing other meals. I had the margherita la trinita pizza, how can you go to an Italian Restaurant and not have a pizza, I have had many margherita pizza before, but this pizza is just a cut above the rest, The base is thick and the crust is so crispy and so soft when you bite into it. The cheese just pulls off so smoothly when you bite into a slice and the flavour just explodes in your mouth. This is a must have when you want a pizza.

Oxtailhot & Heavenly Delictably delicious

Then we also had the Oxtailhot & Heavenly, This is meat beefie meat with gravy and mash potatoes. This was divine the beef was soft like it was melting in you mouth, mixed with the mash the combination on flavour is tangy and spicy at the same time, talk about confusing your taste buds.

Fried Fish Taste for the kings

If you looking for a wild experiance you must have the catch of the day. This fish is freshly caught from the river and is preapred on open flames and soaked in amazing sauses, garlic, spicy and tangy flavours, with a side of potjie-veggies...sounds so exciting you have to taste it.
